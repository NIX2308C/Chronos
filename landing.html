How it works

Three steps from an empty class to a tutor your students can trust.

STEP 01

Build the knowledge base

Upload your PDFs, Word files and notes, or simply type in rules the tutor should follow. Each class keeps its own private library.

STEP 02

Students ask away

Your students chat in plain language and get answers drawn from your material. Every reply points back to the source it came from.

STEP 03

You see the gaps

Questions are sorted by topic, and the ones your material could not answer get flagged. You always know what to add next.

Built for classrooms where accuracy matters

The tutor only knows what you give it, so you stay in control of what your students learn.

gpp_good

It will not make things up

If the answer is not in your rules or your documents, the tutor says so plainly. No invented facts, no confident guessing.

Bring your own material

Drop in PDFs, Word docs or typed rules. Every class stays separate, so your chemistry notes never leak into a history lesson.

Know what they are stuck on

Questions are grouped by topic and the unanswered ones are flagged for you. Think of it as a quiet list of what to teach next.

See what your class is really asking

Every question is captured and sorted by topic, so you can tell at a glance what your students keep coming back to and where your material runs out.
