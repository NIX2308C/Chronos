How it Works

Three steps from your course materials to grounded, on-topic answers.

01
upload_file

Teacher adds knowledge

Upload documents or type rules in the Teacher Portal. Each one is converted into a searchable "meaning" and stored.

02
search

Student asks a question

The system finds the most relevant teacher rules for that exact question — by meaning, not just keywords.

03
auto_awesome

AI answers from those rules

Gemini writes an answer using only the retrieved rules, then the exchange is logged for the analytics dashboard.
