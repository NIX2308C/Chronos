How it Works

Three steps from your course materials to grounded, on-topic answers.

01
upload_file

Teacher sets up a class

Create a class, upload documents or type rules into it, and share the join code. Each class keeps its own material — nothing is mixed together.

02
search

Student joins and asks

A student joins with the code, then asks away. Chronos pulls the pieces of that class's material that actually match the question — by meaning, not just keywords.

03
auto_awesome

It answers from those pieces

The tutor writes its answer from only those matched pieces. If nothing matches, it says so — and that question shows up in your analytics as a gap to fill.
